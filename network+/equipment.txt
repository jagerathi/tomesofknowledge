=== NETWORKING EQUIPMENT ===

HUB 

    * connects multiple devices 
    * incoming frames are copies and broadcasted to all connected devices 

MAC (Media Address Control)

    * 48 bits - 8 hexadecimal pairs
    * First 6 characters are OEM id
    * Last 6 characters are the unique id 

ROUTER 

    * At least 2 connections 
    * Often combined with a switch 
    * used to connect multiple local networks
