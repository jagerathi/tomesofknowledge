=== TOPOLOGIES ===

LOGICAL 

    * The electronic flow of frames 

BUS 

    * All devices connect on a single cable 

RING 

    * Single ring of cabling. No ends. 

STAR 

    * Individual hosts plugged into a block in the middle 

HYBRID STAR-BUS 

    * Bus is replaced with Hub / Switch 
    * Devices connect to the "bus" in a star pattern 
    * The primary topology that is used today 

MESH

    * Unique to wireless networks 
    * All hosts connected directly to each other 
    * Can be fully meshed or partially meshed
