*** Refining a web search ***

    Search for a specific site: site:nytimes.com (no spaces)
    Search for a related site: related:time.com 
    Search for a price: camera $400
    Search hashtages: #knollitall 
    Exclude words: jaguar speed -car 
    Exact match: "tallest building"
    Range of numbers: camera $50..$100
