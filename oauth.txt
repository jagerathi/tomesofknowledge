=== OAUTH ===

https://oauth.net/

An open protocol to allow secure authorization in a simple and standard method from web, mobile and desktop applications.

OAuth 2.0 is the industry-standard protocol for authorization. OAuth 2.0 focuses on client developer simplicity while providing 
specific authorization flows for web applications, desktop applications, mobile phones, and living room devices. 
This specification and its extensions are being developed within the IETF OAuth Working Group.
